Hello and welcome to my little space on the internet. My name is Ara and I am a full-stack web developer and much more. Hope you like my portfolio theme, I was going for a Super Nintendo 16bit theme as its my favorite console and brings back a lot of good memeories for me. I started my web development journey back in mid 2018 at the UCLA full-stack bootcamp where we focused on the M.E.R.N stack. I enjoy working on all sorts of projects whether it be at the front or back end, debugging and automating mundane tasks. After my bootcamp I was able to land a job at TMZ as a QA Analyst. Being in an actual SCRUM/AGILE professional environment I was able to learn a whole lot about the entire development process.

As a QA Analyst I learned a great deal about various testing tools, methodolgies, and automation. From wrting entire test suites and test cases into TestRail to verifying bug fixes or enhancements before reaching production. I was really able to flex my coding skills when it came to automation. Initially when it came to automation our stack consisted of Java and TestNG. After a team discussion we believed that this stack was overkill and not industry standard for web testing. We finally settled on: Gauge, Python and Selenium. We were successfully able to automate several of our test suites and run full end-to-end tests with Slack reporting.

My goal has always been to be a web developer where I can contribute to something just more than tabloid news.
