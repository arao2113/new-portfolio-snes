I enjoy working on all sorts of projects whether it be at the front or back end, debugging and automating mundane tasks. After my bootcamp I was able to land a job at TMZ as a QA Analyst. We finally settled on: Gauge, Python and Selenium. We were successfully able to automate several of our test suites and run full end-to-end tests with Slack reporting.
